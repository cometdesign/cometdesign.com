Welcome to Comet Design, Inc.

Our Services:

Comet Design provides quality Product Development services for the aerospace, biotech, and consumer electronics industries.

We specialize in complicated mechanism design and highly sculpted organic surfacing including aerospace fairing design. We retain experts in the areas of most all of the classic manufacturing processes including injection molding of all kinds, compression molding, progressive die stamping, die casting, investment casting, sand casting, plastic and aluminum extrusions, screw machining, cnc machining, and composites.

We have quick turn prototyping and manufacturing capabilities with our Haas 4 axis CNC machine.
