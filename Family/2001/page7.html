Moab / Arches NP

This was one of our main destinations.  We wanted to check out Moab and Arches National Park.  It's hard to believe we spent a fair amount of time in Utah skiing at Alta and  Snowbird over the years, but never visited Southern Utah.  Moab is the "mountain biking capitol of the world".

Will biked the famous Slickrock trail after only really being on a mountain bike about 4 times.  (Needless to say, Deb declined.  Plenty of other less difficult trails.)  Will survived and feels he was forced to improve quickly.  Clipless petals are painful to get used to.  We both had a few crashes, usually at a stop trying to get a foot released!
