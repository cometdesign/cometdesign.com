On the Road

With good came the bad which is life, I guess.  A transmission radiator fluid leak, a tow truck crunched the back end of the RV, and three tire blow-outs kept the trip challenging.  More than once we thought we should turn back, while we still could.

We had no rain for an entire month of our trip, the remaining days were mostly nice too, except for an early hurricane-force storm in Buffalo NY that stranded us in a Target parking lot.

There were other places we went that did not make it on this web page.  We wanted a little more time to visit with more friends and see more places, but overall the trip was lots of fun and refreshing.

Thanks to everyone for your hospitality.  It was great to see you!  Have a great 2002!
