Crystal Mountain

We did not get season's passes for the first time out here because we thought that Will may not be able to ski as much after his knee injury playing soccer this summer.

The good news is that Will is skiing fine with his new carbon fiber knee brace.   If this keeps up, he may not need surgery.  We are quite a pair with 3 knee braces between the two of us!

The skiing has been fabulous!  We are officially out of the draught.  It has been cool and rainy in Seattle which means lots of snow in the mountains.  The mountains received more than 170" of snowfall in just over two weeks. We already have more snow and better cover in the mountains than we did anytime last year.
