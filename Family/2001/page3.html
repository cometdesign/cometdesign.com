Zion NP

Zion National Park was one of our favorite parks.  The cliffs and rock are amazing.  You can bike into the park which no longer allows any car traffic, only shuttle access or bikes.

We had our favorite camping site ever right on the Virgin River.  The weather at the end of September was a very dry 80 to 90 degrees.  We went swimming in the river at our campsite.  A power site was helpful for air conditioning.  Really roughin' it!

We hiked up the "Narrows" which is mostly river hiking up a narrow corridor in the canyon.  Our ski poles sure helped in the water.

The dogs enjoyed the convertible, and Ellie Mae did not mind hanging in the RV at all.
