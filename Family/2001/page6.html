Canyonlands NP

Canyonlands National Park is in Utah, South of Moab.  We entered the more remote access in what they call the Needles District.  Not many people, but of course this is less than a month after 9/11, so many people may have cancelled their trips.

For some reason this is the one place our cat wanted to take off, perhaps to hunt the strange insects.

Ancient petrogliphs on the rocks made for a historical hike.

We had a nice bike trip here.
