Lake Powell

We were just going to pass through this park called Glen Canyon National Park on the border of Arizona and Utah, but came up on this enormous lake that looked surreal!  We stayed several days, enjoying the dry hot weather, swimming, and renting a boat.  We drove the boat over a 100 miles up to visit Natural Bridges National Monument (the remote gigantic arch above).

This lake (Lake Powell) north of the Grand Canyon still on the Colorado river was dammed, backing up the water in this canyon.  The water is very, very deep with the canyon walls jutting out of the water.   Apparently a controversial dam which greatly changed the river's climate and fish in the Grand Canyon area...but a heck of a recreational paradise.
