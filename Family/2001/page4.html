Grand Canyon NP

We visited the North Rim of the Grand Canyon, which is the more remote place to visit here, compared to the very commercialized South Rim.  Our campsite had no amenities, no cell service, and the only store for miles was a little convenient store.  Elevation is about 8000 feet plus or minus.  You certainly can not be afraid of heights in this park.

Fires were burning nearby which made the pictures a tad smoky.  It was interesting to watch the fire-fighting efforts.  We saw the fire spread.  Permissable fires are fires allowed that start often by natural means, such as this one started by lightning; however, they can get out of control easily if weather is problematic (e.g. high winds).  We were amazed how many of our national parks had fires.
