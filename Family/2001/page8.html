NJ / NYC

It was nice to visit with Will's family in NJ.  We arrived on Mom's birthday!  Somehow we managed to not take many people pictures.  Oh well.

We went to the Jersey shore which is always nice...white sand beaches and usually a good surf.  The Fall scene above is in the back yard of Mom and Dad Stiles' new place.  Just follow a short trail in.

We stayed in a RV campground in Jersey City right on the Hudson, the nearest camping facilities anywhere near NYC.  We took the ferry into NYC about a month after 9/11.  It was a sad, but memorable part of our trip.  We walked for miles to reach sections of subways still operating.  We visited the Empire State Building after waiting in long lines for the limited hours open.  The sunset is one of the views from the top.  Security was tough.  The Statue of Liberty was still closed.  Times Square was interesting.  Our whole trip, which started 9/10 seemed like we were in a new world.  We felt strangely blessed to be enjoying the freedoms of the USA, during such troubling times.
