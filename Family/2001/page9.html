Maine

We spent a week in Maine visiting family while we parked in Norma and Dad's driveway.  We ate lobster of course, and worked on our land on Appleton Ridge.  We cleared some of the trees that had grown over the years blocking the view.  We also went for a ride on Dad's boat to an Island, with not a sole around on a clear, cool and crisp Fall day.

The dogs decided to take off on our land for over 3 hours, worrying us to no end, but they returned at dusk.  They had the time of their life we think.

One of our favorite pictures above is Will Davee and Dave smooching.

We also met up with some friends from Washington (Fred and Betty) in Freeport.  They are now retired, enjoying the RV life.
